10 Free Space Background Images (JPG)

If you’ve ever doubted the beauty of the universe, you probably just haven’t gazed at it with a knowing eye. There’s beauty on every level—simplicity and regularity, even a kind of grace – from the smallest sub-atomic particles to the largest clusters of galaxies. The universe is nothing short of an absolute masterpiece!

Part of the reason why humans have always found the universe fascinating is because of its enigmatic nature – most of us can only ever dream of going to outer space! However, we can satiate our curiosity with beautiful photography, as well as some out-of-the-world designs which take us to another dimension with their galactic effects.

The cosmic effect, which gained popularity in 2018 and has ever since been quite a trend in the realm of graphic design, epitomizes the rapid advancement in technology in the last few decades. If you’re a designer who would love to experiment with this effect and make your designs as intriguing as our night skies are, be our guest!

These 10 free space backgrounds from FreezeronDigital comprise of beautiful images of the night skies in various hues and shades that would elevate your designs to the next level. High-resolution textures have been made available as PNG files, each possessing dimensions 3000 x 2000 px. Not only can you use these images in your digital designs such as websites and apps, they are also print-ready in 300 DPI, so you get to use them in posters, banners, magazines, book cover designs, and pretty much any and every design project that could use a bit of the quiet beauty that the cosmos have to offer!
